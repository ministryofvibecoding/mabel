Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

To create your account, we store your email address and the habit data you enter — jar names, marble counts, and dates. This is stored securely using Supabase.

What we don't collect

We don't collect your name, location, device identifiers, or any personal information beyond what's needed to run your account. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with third parties.

Motion data

If you enable tilt and shake, your device motion is used locally to animate the marbles. This data is never sent anywhere.

Account deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us here.
